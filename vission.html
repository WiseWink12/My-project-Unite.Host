Our Vision / Goal

The vision of UNITE is to create a peaceful, inclusive, and united Kiziba Refugee Camp where every individual is treated with dignity and respect.

We aim to eliminate discrimination and ethnocentrism by building strong unity among all groups, regardless of culture, language, or background.

Diversity should not divide people — it should bring strength, understanding, and cooperation.

Long-Term Impact

UNITE envisions a future where youth and adults work together in harmony, build friendships across groups, and promote peace in all daily interactions.

This will create a stronger, safer, and more united community for everyone in Kiziba Camp.

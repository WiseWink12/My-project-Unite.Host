Welcome to UNITE

UNITE is a youth-led community initiative based in Kiziba Refugee Camp. It promotes unity, peace, and cultural respect among all residents regardless of identity, language, or background.

The project responds to existing divisions among groups such as Banyamulenge, Banyamasisi, and Bazahirwa, where misunderstanding and stereotypes have sometimes affected peaceful coexistence.

UNITE aims to replace division with dialogue, respect, cooperation, and a shared identity as one community.

Our Purpose

The purpose of UNITE is to build a peaceful and inclusive environment where everyone feels respected and valued equally.
